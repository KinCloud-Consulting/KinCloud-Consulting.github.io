Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-16

1. Purpose

Internal QBO App is an internal tool used by Barton Integration LLC to read accounting data from our own QuickBooks Online tenant for internal usage.

2. Data We Access

  • Accounting entities exposed by the QuickBooks Online API (e.g., invoices, customers, chart of accounts).
  • OAuth 2.0 access and refresh tokens required to authenticate.

3. Data Storage & Retention

  • Access tokens are processed only for the purpose of internal tools & workflows.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share or sell any QuickBooks data—or user‑identifiable information—to third parties.

5. Security

  • All API traffic is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

6. Usage

The app is used solely by Company staff on Company data.

We may process business contact information contained in QBO records, used only for internal operations.

7. Changes

We may update this policy and will post any changes on this page with a new effective date.
